Welcome to the nuACM election application. Here you can gather information on front-runners of the next nuACM election, and on election day, vote for your favorite candidate.

If you wish to run for an office in nuACM, please log into our website using your CCIS credentials. After doing so, you will see a link where you logged in for Becoming a Candidate. Fill out your personal information, including choosing a small profile photo and blurb about yourself, as well as choosing the office(s) you wish to run for, and submit the form. From then on, you may access this profile by logging into this site and clicking the Edit my Profile link where you log in.
